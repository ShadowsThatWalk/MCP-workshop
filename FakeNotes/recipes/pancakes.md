# Quick Pancakes

Ingredients:

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp baking powder

Steps:

1. Mix dry ingredients.
2. Add milk and egg, whisk until smooth.
3. Fry on medium heat until golden.
